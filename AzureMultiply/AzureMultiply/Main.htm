Welcome to Azure Math!

Azure Math is a site aimed at helping children aged 8 to 12 years to become proficient in arithmetic - times tables, divide tables, bonds from 2 - 15.

It allows the children to challenge themselves and others, scoring points in different categories for speed and accuracy.

The site can be used without registering, but the real fun begins once you are registered and can challenge yourself agaist your past scores, and your friends.
